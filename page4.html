Mission

To create an environment open for anyone who seeks comfort in peers.

Vision

Our vision is that peer support is a viable option for anyone who wants the mutuality of a compassionate peer support relationship.

How to Participate or Help?

You can help by simply informing or alerting the peer facis if ever there is someone in need of assistance. You can also approach the guidance unit if ever!!
